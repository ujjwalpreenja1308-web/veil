# Slack

Connect Slack to receive failure alerts directly in your channel.

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings** in your Veil dashboard
2. Click **Connect Slack**
3. Authorize Veil in the Slack OAuth flow (this adds the Veil bot to your workspace)
4. Invite the Veil bot to any channel you want alerts in: `/invite @Veil`
5. That is it

You will see a welcome message in your channel confirming the connection.

## What you receive

When a session fails, Veil sends a Slack message with:

- The failure category and severity
- A plain-language reason for the failure
- A direct link to the session in the dashboard

## Changing the alert channel

Invite the Veil bot to a different channel with `/invite @Veil` and remove it from the old one. Alerts follow the bot.

## Disconnecting

To remove the Slack integration, go to **Settings** and click **Disconnect** next to the Slack section. Alerts stop immediately.

## Troubleshooting

**No messages arriving**

- Check that the Slack connection shows as active in Settings
- Make sure you invited the Veil bot to your channel with `/invite @Veil`
- Check that your agent is actually triggering failures by looking at the Sessions page

**Messages going to the wrong channel**

Invite the Veil bot to the correct channel and remove it from the old one.
